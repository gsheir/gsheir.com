About me

I’m a versatile data science consultant, who delivers tangible results in high pressure environments across elite sport, healthcare and the wider consulting industry, and am passionate about using cutting-edge analytics to raise elite sport to new heights.

Recent highlights:

  • Built a operating theatre scheduling and patient tracking dashboard for the NHS which increased the number of patients treated by 1200 in 3 months.
  • Rolled out the dashboard to 2 other NHS Trusts, liaising with NHS users, technical and non-technical peers.
  • Increased the precision of a smart beard trimmer by 100x, using computer vision and machine learning on a microcontroller, as software lead in a multidisciplinary engineering team that included optics, mechanical and electronic engineers.
  • Upskilled 40+ NATO operatives in Digital and Data technologies through classroom training and workshops.
  • Pitched and won £50k of investment in a hackathon with the British Army to develop a next-gen casualty management app.

Alongside my day job in data and software consulting, I am the Men's 1st Team Performance Analyst at Richmond Hockey Club, performing both live and post-match analysis in the 2nd tier of England Hockey League. I am also an England Hockey Level 2 coach.
